My Plans

My Goals and Aspirations

Current Goals/Next Few Years

First and foremost I want to graduate college. I want to get a job in the field of Computer Science. I want to get my career started.
Inbetween that I got a few things to work on. I want to get my own personal projects finished, and then get them together in some sort of portfolio.
Actually having tangible evidence of my progress and work I could show people would help me immensely.

I also hope to work on my own social skills. I want to be able to talk to people better on a more professional and causal level.
I would like to stop worrying about how I come off to people and know that I am being a good person when talking to someone.
Less confusion, and no more grave mistakes.

Future Goals/Next 20 Years

I want to be the big fish on top. The large kahuna. A man with control over a large amount of things.
I want to the be one in control so I can change things for the better. There are many terrible practices at play in gaming companies today.
I want to change that. I want to see those bad things get weeded out in my lifetime. That is my biggest goal.

Other than that I would like to have several big games in my name. Have worked on instant classics, horrible flops, and the most mediocre crap on the market.
I want to be well rounded and experienced. Have a good reason to be at the place I am. I don't know everything right now. I want to learn. I want to do better than those at the top now
