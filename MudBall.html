Mud Ball

This game was created in class for an assignment over a few days.
The assignment was to apply our Unity knowledge to create a Ball Roller game.


Being honest, I do not think this project holds up to any of my standards.
This comes from me not understanding the scope of what I was creating,
and not being able to give myself enough time that I needed.
I take this as a learning moment to more accuretly scope my future projects.


What I did learn from this project was a more in-depth understanding of Unity's Physics System.
